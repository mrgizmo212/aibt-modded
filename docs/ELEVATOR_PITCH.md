## Response to Common Questions

**Q: "How is this different from normal backtesting?"**  
A: Traditional backtesting tests if fixed rules worked. We let AI interpret your goals and explain why it made each decision. You can chat with it afterward: "Why did you exit early?" and get real answers.

**Q: "Can the AI do whatever it wants?"**  
A: No—you set hard constraints (order types, shorting, margin) that are strictly enforced. The AI operates within your rules, using them as guardrails while pursuing your strategic goals.

**Q: "What if I want to compare different approaches?"**  
A: Create multiple AI models with different personalities—same strategy but GPT-5 vs Claude vs Gemini. Or same AI but different parameters (temperature for risk tolerance). Track and compare all of them.

**Q: "Is this for real trading?"**  
A: Currently focused on backtesting and simulation. You learn from AI decision-making, understand what works, then can apply those insights to live trading through your broker.

**Q: "Do I need to code?"**  
A: No. Describe your strategy in plain English: "Focus on high-volume breakouts, exit on weakness." The AI interprets and executes. Technical users can add custom rules, but it's optional.
